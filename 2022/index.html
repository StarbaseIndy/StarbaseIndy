    Authors

    E. S. Fein (Author)

    E. S. Fein is the author of two novels (A Dream of Waking Life and Points of Origin) and two short story collections (Ascendescenscion and The Process is Love). His favorite genres to read and write are science fiction, speculative fiction, and thrillers. More than anything, he enjoys writing strange stories about strange characters in strange situations. Some people refer to him as a lucid dreaming expert. Others know him as a rapper. But E. S. Fein just thinks of himself as a dude who likes to think about what it means to be a dude thinking about being a dude. When he isn't hanging out with his partner and son, you can usually find him reading, playing DnD with his friends, going for walks while writing stories in his head before putting them on paper, or practicing yoga.

    On offer: books, stickers, mugs, and shirts

    Michael L. Romansky (Author)

    Home grown Indianapolis author who love Sci-fi and is stepping into the convention selling circuit for the first time. Michael grew up going to Starbase Indy and is anxious for the chance to return.

    On offer: Six separate titles available in hard cover and paperback.
    https://www.amazon.com/autor/michaelromansky

    Sara Noë (Author)

    Sara A. Noë is an award-winning author, artist, and photographer from La Porte, Indiana. The first two books in her paranormal sci-fan series both received the Literary Titan Gold Book Award. Sara has had poetry, short fiction, and memoir works featured in various anthologies and journals since 2005, and several of her poems are available in the Indiana Poetry Archives. Notable art achievements include her photograph "Aftermath" published on the cover of Voices Literary Journal in 2018, photograph "Spiraling" awarded Best Still Life in a 2019 juried gallery exhibit, and artwork "The Raven" and "Dapple Gray" currently on display in the Uptown Arts District of Michigan City, Indiana. As an artist, Sara creates her own covers and graphics. She lives in a little cottage with her calico cat, Calypso, who has a fondness for chewing pens and being as loud and sassy as possible.

    On offer: Books, art and photography prints, candles, cosplay animal ear headbands and hair clips, puzzles, notecards, postcards, stickers, and buttons.

    T.C. Pulley (Author)

    Home grown Indianapolis author who love Sci-fi and is stepping into the convention selling circuit for the first time. T.C. Pulley is excited for the chance to learn and grow among one of the greatest fandoms of the Sci-Fi universe.

    On offer: Two separate titles available in hard cover and paperback.
    https://www.amazon.com/author/t.c.pulley

    Art by Jama

    Jama Young is a spiritual artist from Indiana. She has a BFA in Fine Arts from IU East with a concentration in ceramic art. She has sold her artwork all over the United States, including a sculpture that is part of the permanent collection in the RIchmond Museum of Art. Jama is the creator of prayer urns, which are sculpted vessels used in prayer and/or meditation.

    On offer: Prayer urns - These are sculpted pieces of art used in prayer, meditation, and reiki/yoga. They are non-denominational and faith-inclusive. The urns are mainly fantasy-themed, but also feature astrological, astronomical, and and geometric designs as well as animals and landscapes.
